# Strategy: Earnings Revision Acceleration

## Economic Rationale
Analyst earnings revisions often exhibit momentum due to information diffusion delays and cognitive biases (anchoring, herding). Companies experiencing accelerating positive earnings revisions represent situations where fundamental improvement is being recognized by the analyst community, but this information takes time to be fully incorporated into stock prices due to investor attention constraints and gradual updating of expectations.
